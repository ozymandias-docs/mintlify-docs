# Welcome to Continia Banking

Continia Banking is a comprehensive solution integrated with Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central, designed to streamline and automate payments and reconciliation processes. It connects Business Central directly to your bank, allowing for various payment methods and automating tasks like cash receipt journal lines and reconciliation suggestions. This automation reduces errors and enhances efficiency.

Embedded within Business Central, Continia Banking offers an intuitive user experience for both new and experienced users. Its straightforward setup and integration process, along with a wide range of features, support and optimize your financial operations.

For an overview of the available modules and their features, read the [Business Functionality Continia Banking](@CB-144) article.
